Dino Growth Explorer: Ancient Isles Reborn

The spirit of the old game is still here: grow from hatchling to titan, cross ancient biomes, solve shrine riddles, hunt or graze your way forward, and break through relic-locked gates. The difference is the world is now rendered as a real low-poly 3D island chain.

WASD Move through the isles in full 3D.
Shift Burst forward and spend sprint energy.
F Attack creatures and strike shrine nodes.
Space Pulse nearby food, shrines, and secrets.
Esc Pause or resume the run.

This version uses a low-poly Three.js scene loaded from a CDN. If the 3D engine fails to load, the game will not boot. Choose a species to drop into Ancient Isles Reborn.
