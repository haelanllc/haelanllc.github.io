Dino Growth Explorer: Ancient Isles Reborn

Start as a tiny hatchling and grow into an ancient titan across a handcrafted island chain inspired by the feeling of a top-down Zelda adventure. Choose a land, sea, or sky dinosaur, learn what it eats, clear shrines, unlock sealed gates with relics, explore hidden sea caves, fight rival apex predators, and uncover pirate treasure in a world built for discovery first.

Grow with intent: stage upgrades take longer now, so every new tier feels earned instead of automatic.
Solve real shrines: puzzles mix Step, Space pulse, timed relays, and combat trials.
Adventure rules: relics from shrines power the late gates, so exploration matters as much as XP.
Controls: WASD move, Shift sprint, F attack, Space pulse, Esc pause.
